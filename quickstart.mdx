# Quick Start

Follow these steps to install Helix Flight Planner and create your first aerial survey mission.

## Step 1: Download the App

Visit [app.helix.aero/download](https://app.helix.aero/download) and select your platform.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    Download the **Helix-FlightPlanner-Setup-x64.exe** installer for Windows 10 or later (64-bit).

    ![Download page — Windows selected](/images/quickstart-download-windows.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="macOS">
    Download the **Helix-FlightPlanner-macOS.dmg** disk image for macOS 12 Monterey or later.

    ![Download page — macOS selected](/images/quickstart-download-macos.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 2: Install and Launch

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    1. Run the downloaded `.exe` installer.
    2. Follow the installation wizard (default path: `C:\Program Files\Helix Flight Planner`).
    3. Launch **Helix Flight Planner** from the Start menu or desktop shortcut.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="macOS">
    1. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
    2. Drag **Helix Flight Planner** to your Applications folder.
    3. Launch the app from Applications or Spotlight.

    <Note>
      On first launch, macOS may show a security prompt. The app is notarized by Apple — click **Open** to proceed.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Log In

On the login screen, enter your Helix Aero credentials.

![Login screen](/images/quickstart-login.png)

If you don't have an account yet, visit [app.helix.aero](https://app.helix.aero) to register or request a trial license.

## Step 4: License Activation

License activation happens automatically on your first login. The app sends your machine fingerprint to the Helix Aero licensing server.

- **Success:** A confirmation message appears briefly and you are taken to the dashboard.
- **Slot limit exceeded:** If your account has reached the maximum number of registered machines, a message appears with a link to manage your machines at [app.helix.aero](https://app.helix.aero).

<Note>
  Your license allows offline use for up to 7 days. After 7 days without a network connection, the app enters read-only mode until it can re-verify online.
</Note>

## Step 5: Create Your First Project

1. From the **Dashboard**, click **New Project**.
2. Enter a project name (e.g., "Survey Zone A — April 2026").
3. Click **Create**. The project opens in the mission planning view.

![Dashboard with new project button](/images/quickstart-dashboard.png)

## Step 6: Create a Mission

1. Inside your project, click **New Mission**.
2. On the 3D globe, draw your **Area of Interest (AOI)**:
   - Click to place polygon vertices.
   - Close the polygon by clicking the first point or pressing Enter.
3. Select a **sensor** from the catalog panel on the right.
4. Adjust flight parameters: altitude, overlap, sidelap, heading.
5. The app immediately computes flight strips and displays them on the globe.

![Mission planning view with AOI and flight strips](/images/quickstart-mission.png)
