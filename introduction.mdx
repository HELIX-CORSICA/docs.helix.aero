# Helix Flight Planner

Helix Flight Planner is a professional desktop application for planning aerial survey missions. Designed for photogrammetry, LiDAR and pushbroom operators, it combines a live 3D globe, accurate strip computation, terrain-aware altitude modes, quality control overlays and multi-format export in a single, cross-platform interface.

## Supported Sensors

Helix Flight Planner ships with a catalog of **80+ cameras**, **14+ LiDAR sensors** and a library of aircraft profiles, all curated by Helix Aero. You can also define custom sensors and aircraft with full specification control.

- **Cameras** — focal length, sensor dimensions, pixel size, forward overlap, sidelap and GSD at any altitude.
- **LiDAR** — FOV, pulse repetition rate, scan rate, beam divergence, swath width and point density.
- **Pushbroom** — line sensor geometry and native strip spacing rules.

Major manufacturers covered include Sony, Phase One, Hasselblad, DJI, MicaSense, Leica, Trimble, PhaseOne iXM, Velodyne, Ouster, Livox, Riegl, YellowScan and more.

## Supported Altitude Modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **AMSL** | Absolute altitude above Mean Sea Level. Classic fixed-altitude planning. |
| **iAMSL** | Initial AMSL — altitude interpreted relative to mean sea level on the DEM. |
| **iAGL** | Initial AGL — altitude interpreted above ground level, strips follow the DEM to maintain constant terrain clearance. |

The DEM source is selected per mission in the wizard (SRTM 30 m, Copernicus GLO-30, or none for flat terrain).
